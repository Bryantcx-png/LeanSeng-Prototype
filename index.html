联成鱼行 — Lean Seng Frozen Sea Product

We are a family-run frozen seafood trading business based in Penang, Malaysia. For years, we have been supplying fresh, high-quality frozen seafood to restaurants, F&B businesses, wholesale buyers, and households across the region.

Our products are sourced directly from trusted suppliers and handled with strict cold-chain standards — so every piece of seafood you receive is as fresh as it can be.

We serve customers in English, 中文, and Bahasa Malaysia, and offer both delivery and self-pickup options for your convenience.
